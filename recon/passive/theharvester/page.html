theHarvester
https://github.com/laramies/theHarvester

Email and company enumeration tool


Switches

-h use SHODAN database to query discovered hosts


Examples

theharvester -d <seznam.cz> -b all


Notes

Collects information about domain or company from
  • google
  • googleCSE
  • bing
  • bingapi
  • pgp
  • linkedin
  • google-profiles
  • jigsaw
  • twitter
  • googleplus
  • all
